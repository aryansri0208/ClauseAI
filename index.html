Frequently Asked Questions

Is ClauseAI legal advice?

No. ClauseAI is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. We offer informational analysis and market benchmarking to help you understand SaaS Terms of Service and make informed decisions. For high-stakes contracts, custom agreements, or situations requiring legal interpretation, you should consult with a licensed attorney.

Who is ClauseAI for?

ClauseAI is built for startup founders, CTOs, engineers, and operations teams who manage SaaS subscriptions without full-time legal support. If you're signing up for tools like Slack, Notion, AWS, Stripe, or HubSpot and want to understand what you're agreeing to, ClauseAI is for you.

What types of contracts does ClauseAI support?

ClauseAI is SaaS-only. We analyze standard Terms of Service and Privacy Policies for subscription software products.

We support: Cloud hosting, developer tools and APIs, productivity software, CRM and sales tools, analytics and marketing platforms, and other standard SaaS subscriptions.

We do NOT support: Custom enterprise MSAs, employment contracts, vendor services agreements, real estate or lease agreements, NDAs, or non-software contracts of any kind.

How is ClauseAI different from other contract tools?

Most contract analysis tools are built for legal teams reviewing enterprise deals. ClauseAI is built specifically for startup founders managing SaaS subscriptions. We focus exclusively on SaaS Terms of Service, benchmark every term against market norms, and track contracts over time so you never miss a renewal or price change.
